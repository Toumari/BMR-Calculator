    How did we calculate this figure?

    The BMR is the amount of calories your body needs to function properly. It is the amount of calories you need to consume to maintain your current weight. The BMR is calculated using the Mifflin-St Jeor equation.

    The equation is as follows:

    BMR = 10 x weight (kg) + 6.25 x height (cm) - 5 x age (years) + 5
